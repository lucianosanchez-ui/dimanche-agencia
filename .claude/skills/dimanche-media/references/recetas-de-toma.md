# Recetas de toma (Branded AI Guide → Dimanche)

> Cada tipo de toma sigue el mismo patrón: **lente + apertura concretos · plano · capas de profundidad (qué está sharp / qué blurred) · dirección y calidad de luz · paleta explícita · mood al cierre.** Los prompts del PDF (abajo, verbatim, en inglés) son **puntos de partida para aprender el patrón, NO para copiar literal**. Después de cada categoría hay una versión **adaptada a Dimanche** (panadería/food). Recordá la regla de oro: lo identificable real se compone encima (ver `postproduccion-y-realismo.md`).

## Toma de modelo
Patrón: capas (FG/MG/BG), lente 70–85mm f/2–2.8, luz natural o softbox, paleta explícita, mood editorial.

**Fashion editorial (verbatim):**
> A cinematic fashion editorial photograph with clear depth layers. Foreground: soft, out-of-focus stone or architectural elements subtly framing the composition. Middle ground: a woman in sharp focus, centered, walking up a wide staircase... Shot with an 85mm lens at f/2.0 for shallow depth of field, natural soft light, elegant color contrast... Editorial, minimal, and luxurious mood.

**Swimwear editorial (verbatim, extracto):**
> ...full body shot, wide scene... skin has a peachy, healthy glow with a dewy finish, subtle water droplets visible... Shot with a Canon EOS R5 using an 85mm f/1.8 lens at f/2.2 for soft controlled blur and sharp focus on skin detail...

Para Dimanche: solo **gente anónima** (manos, siluetas, comensales no identificables). Nunca el equipo real.

## Toma de producto
Patrón: macro/short-tele (90–100mm) f/4–8, ángulo definido, FG sharp / BG blurred, luz difusa lateral, mood "refined, tactile".

**Earrings on textured surface (verbatim, extracto):**
> A minimal, high-end jewelry product shot captured with a 90mm macro lens at f/4.0... Camera angle: directly front-facing at eye level... Foreground (sharp): ... on a rough white stone or textured plaster surface... Background (blurred): smooth, warm-grey backdrop, matte finish... Lighting: soft, diffused natural light from the left, minimal shadow... Mood: refined, calm, and tactile.

**Beauty product (verbatim, extracto):**
> ...She holds a minimalist white skincare bottle vertically next to her face... realistic pores, slight texture, natural undertones, not overly airbrushed... one large softbox from above and front-left (45°)... warm and diffused, emulating early morning studio light. Background warm neutral grey, shallow depth of field. Shot on a Canon EOS R6.

## Producto 3D / render
Patrón: "ultra-realistic product mockup / 3D rendering", estudio minimalista, condensación/gotas para frescura, key + rim light, 50mm f/3.5.

**Beauty product render (verbatim, extracto):**
> Ultra-realistic product mockup of a sleek cylindrical tube with a soft matte finish, floating mid-air in a minimalist studio... surface covered in cold condensation droplets... soft key light from front-left, subtle rim light from back-right... Shot with a Sony A7R III and 50mm f/1.8 lens at f/3.5... High-end beauty brand campaign style.

Útil para Dimanche solo para conceptos/mock genéricos (ej. packaging tipo, no el real). El producto real → foto real.

## Toma de escena
Patrón: 24–35mm f/2.5–4, contexto ambiental, motion blur opcional, persona en un tercio, "the product feels naturally embedded, not hero-shot".

**Urban fashion (verbatim, extracto):**
> A high-fashion urban scene with a 35mm lens at f/2.5... Foreground: slight motion blur of a passing vehicle... Middle ground (in focus): a woman walks briskly across a crosswalk... Natural daylight, soft and even... The product (bag) is not hero-shot but treated as part of the movement and styling flow.

**Outdoor editorial (verbatim, extracto):**
> A cinematic outdoor fashion scene with a 35mm lens at f/2.8... camera low and tilted slightly upward... Foreground (sharp): a model close to a large rock formation... Background (soft blur): beige rock formations, pale blue sky... Natural directional light from the left, late afternoon... Mood: effortless, strong, grounded.

## Toma de textura
Patrón: macro 70–100mm f/3.2–8, crop muy cerrado, "surface detail and realism", luz lateral cálida, grano analógico, mood "intimate, tactile".

**Skincare detail (verbatim, extracto):**
> A raw, intimate beauty texture shot with a 100mm macro lens at f/3.2... tightly cropped... thick layer of green exfoliating mask applied unevenly... tiny particles visible, catching light... warm, directional side light creates dimensional shadows... the grain adds an analog feel, emphasizing realism.

**Skincare flat lay (verbatim, extracto):**
> Ultra-detailed flatlay of three textures arranged in a perfectly vertical line on a soft beige background... soft and diffused light from a 45-degree angle... Captured with a Canon EOS R5 with a 50mm macro lens at f/4 for rich detail and softly blurred edges.

## Adaptación Dimanche (food)

Construidas con las 6 capas + paleta azul/crema (sin naranja) + mood warm/homey/Sunday. **Generás la ESCENA con IA; la foto REAL del pan/factura se compone encima (Nano Banana/Canva).** Por eso estos prompts describen el set/fondo/luz, con un placeholder del producto.

**Food studio / product hero (escena para componer el producto real):**
> Food studio shot of an empty cream-toned ceramic plate on a flour-dusted light wood table, soft linen napkin in muted blue to the side, a few scattered crumbs catching the light. Background: warm neutral beige, softly blurred. Soft diffused window light from the left, gentle shadows, warm homey Sunday morning atmosphere. Shot on a Canon EOS R5 with a 100mm macro lens at f/4, shallow depth of field. Editorial food photography, photorealistic, no text, no logo. — *(luego: componer la foto real de la factura/pan sobre el plato).* 

**Product in context / cozy interior (mood de domingo):**
> Cozy interior scene, a warm breakfast table near a window with morning light, steaming coffee cup in soft focus, open book, muted blue and cream palette, flour-dusted wooden surface, linen textures. 35mm lens at f/2.8, natural directional light, atmospheric haze, intimate and homey mood. Photorealistic, no text. — *(componer producto real).* 

**Flat lay (cenital para mostrador/promo):**
> Top-down flat lay on a soft cream linen background, negative space for product, scattered flour and a few wheat stalks, a small ceramic dish with a blue rim, soft even overhead light, gentle shadows, warm minimal Sunday aesthetic. 50mm macro lens at f/5.6. Photorealistic, no text, no logo. — *(componer productos reales en la zona de negative space).* 

**Textura (macro de miga/corteza — solo si es producto genérico, o sobre foto real con Adobe):**
> Extreme macro texture shot of golden bread crust, light raking across the surface revealing crackle and air pockets, warm side light, fine film grain, shallow depth of field, 100mm macro at f/3.5, tactile and appetizing. Photorealistic, no text. — *(para producto identificable, mejor macro de la foto REAL retocada en Adobe).* 

**Video (mood/b-roll, 3-5s, Higgsfield sobre imagen base):**
> Start from a Dimanche food image. Higgsfield, subtle camera move: slow push-in (dolly) toward the plate, gentle steam rising, soft morning light flicker. `silent`, no people talking. 9:16, 3s, warm homey mood. — *(empezá por imagen base; ver `herramientas.md`).* 

> **Siempre:** prompt en inglés, cierre `photorealistic, no text, no logo`; texto y logo se ponen en Canva; lo identificable real se compone, no se genera.
